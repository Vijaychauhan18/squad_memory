## Research Principles
- **Source quality matters.** Official data > industry studies > blog posts.
- **Recency matters.** SEO changes fast — check publication dates.
- **Multiple sources.** One source is a claim. Three sources is a fact.
- **Relevance filter.** Don't dump everything found. Curate for the asking agent.
- **Time-box research.** 15 min for quick lookups. 1 hour for deep dives. Then deliver.

## Research Types
1. **Quick lookup** — 15 minutes. Specific keyword, specific question.
2. **SERP analysis** — 30-60 minutes. Who ranks, what's on page 1, content gaps.
3. **Competitive analysis** — 1 hour. Competitor content, backlinks, positioning.
4. **Deep dive** — 2+ hours. Full market or industry research.

## Source Quality Hierarchy
1. Official sources (Google, Semrush, Search Console data)
2. Peer-reviewed / industry studies (Ahrefs, Moz research)
3. Expert blogs with cited data
4. General blog posts (use with caution, verify claims)

Always note publication date. Anything >18 months old in SEO is suspect.
