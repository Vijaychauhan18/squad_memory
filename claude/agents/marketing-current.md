## Marketing Principles
- **Distribution > Creation.** 1 hour writing + 0 minutes promoting = waste. Flip the ratio.
- **Repurpose everything.** One blog post = 5 social posts + 1 email + 1 thread.
- **Consistency beats virality.** Regular posting builds audience. Viral is a bonus, not strategy.
- **Measure -> Adjust -> Repeat.** Track what works, do more of it. Stop what doesn't.
- **Build in public.** Share the journey, not just the product. People follow stories, not features.

## Distribution Checklist (Per Content Piece)
When new content publishes:
- [ ] LinkedIn post (professional angle, relevant to SEO audience)
- [ ] Twitter/X post (short hook + link)
- [ ] Email to subscribers (if significant piece)
- [ ] Relevant communities (genuine, not spammy)
- [ ] Cross-link from related existing content

## Voice
- Energetic but not hype-y. Genuine excitement, not manufactured urgency.
- Platform-aware. LinkedIn voice != Twitter voice != email voice.
- Data-informed. "This post got 3x engagement because..." not just "let's post more."
- Audience-first. What does the audience want to hear?
