Looking for SEO AI tools to improve your process and workflows? Mike King shares 24 ways he's using AI plus custom GPTs you can start using now.

## Extracted Body
As an SEO professional, I’ve been overwhelmed by the sheer number of SEO AI tools flooding the market. It’s become nearly impossible to identify which ones solve SEO problems.

That’s why I’ve taken the time to test and curate a list of AI tools that have improved my SEO process. Whether you’re looking for no-code tools, SEO automation, or AI to help you become more efficient, you’ll find something you can add to your workflow from my list.

During the webinar, Chima Mmeje mentioned Moz AI as an incredible resource for keyword research and search intent analysis. According to Chima, the tool helps you understand the keywords a website ranks for and the intent behind each search query, making it easier to develop content strategies that align with what searchers are looking for.

Moz AI stands out because of its unique two-layer model for identifying search intent . The first layer is an ML classification model trained on search intent data, and the second layer is a rules-based model built on years of research and expertise about Google SERP signals . This dual approach ensures that the intent classification is accurate and actionable.

When creating in-depth personas, I combine the power of ChatGPT with data from SparkToro . SparkToro provides rich audience insights, including demographic information, online behaviors, and social media activity, which I feed into ChatGPT to generate highly detailed and actionable personas.

Understanding your competitors’ product strategies and audience engagement is crucial for staying ahead. Competely offers a solution by providing high-level competitive analysis focusing on product features, marketing positioning, and audience sentiment.

Understanding user behavior is essential for improving both UX and SEO . I rely on userevaluation for qualitative analysis and insights into how users interact with my site. It pinpoints friction points and optimizes the user journey to improve overall performance.

uxsniff is an AI alternative to HotJar that provides comprehensive insights into how users interact with my site. It combines heatmaps, session recordings, and AI analytics to optimize user experience and boost SEO.

When I talk about taking SEO to the next level, I do that by integrating Screaming Frog SEO Spider with custom JavaScript and vector embeddings . This combination helps with content mapping, internal linking, and keyword relevance analysis while leveraging machine learning.

A major challenge with AI-generated content is the risk of inaccuracies or hallucinations. WordLift addresses this with a fact-checking API that ensures AI-generated content is reliable and factual.

Organizing keywords and content into relevant clusters is critical for building a cohesive SEO strategy. BERTopic uses advanced AI to group keywords and content into meaningful topical clusters, ensuring better search performance and a more structured content plan.

Creating a content marketing strategy takes time, but Content Codex simplifies the process by using AI to generate first drafts. It pulls data analysis, keyword research, and content recommendations, helping me align strategy with business goals.

With prompting, I strongly encourage you to write your prompts instead of using someone else’s, so they are tailored to your use case.

Writing meta descriptions can be time-consuming, especially when managing large-scale content updates.

ChatGPT excels at naturally integrating keywords into existing content, streamlining the optimization process.

Adding internal and external links can be time-consuming, especially for long-form content. ChatGPT makes this task easier by identifying the best link insertion points based on the content's context.

Google’s algorithm tracks how often content is updated and uses this data to influence crawl frequency and rankings. To ensure that my content signals to Google that it’s regularly refreshed, I use ChatGPT to generate TL;DR summaries that I add to my pages. These summaries give an updated overview of the content without requiring a complete rewrite.

Creating content briefs can slow the content creation process, especially when managing multiple SEO projects. ChatGPT helps me quickly generate draft briefs, streamlining this step.

Understanding your audience is critical to any successful SEO or content strategy, and building detailed personas is a vital part of that process. With ChatGPT, I streamline persona modeling by feeding it research data, audience insights, and market reports, which it then organizes into actionable personas.

Generate audience insights: I input demographic data, behavioral traits, and pain points into ChatGPT, which it uses to create detailed personas, including customer goals and challenges.

Tailored communication styles : ChatGPT helps define the preferred communication styles of different personas, making it easier to create audience-specific content.

ChatGPT is phenomenal at optimizing page titles. Instead of manually writing page titles for every page, I automatically use ChatGPT to generate numerous variations.

Creating content that’s easy to read is essential for user experience and SEO. Flesch Kincaid Genius simplifies this by rewriting content to match specific readability levels while preserving its core message.

Manually creating customer personas takes time and can miss critical insights. GPT Persona AI speeds up the process by generating comprehensive, data-driven personas from your audience research.

Maintaining a consistent brand voice is essential for any content strategy, especially when dealing with multiple contributors or automated content. The brand voice and tone review custom GPT ensures that all generated content adheres to a brand’s specific voice and tone guidelines.

Creating schema markup for every webpage can be tedious and prone to errors. The Schema Markup Generator custom GPT simplifies this process by automatically generating structured data for my pages.

Internal linking is critical for SEO, but determining the best link placements can be tedious. DejanSEO’s LinkBERT uses AI to predict link placement, optimizing link equity and user experience.

Managing internal linking across a large site can be daunting, but InLinks automates the process of optimizing internal links based on content relevancy. This ensures my internal links are effective for SEO and user experience without manual effort.

Link Whisper is an AI WordPress plugin that automatically finds and inserts relevant internal links throughout my site. It ensures that my content is well-connected and optimized for users and search engines. It also works with Shopify, making it a versatile tool for different platforms.

Respona streamlines link-building and outreach . It combines AI with data analysis to find relevant opportunities, create personalized outreach campaigns, and manage the entire process.

With so many AI tools available, trying them all is tempting. Instead, focus on those that automate tasks and improve your efficiency. The right tool feels like an extension of you, helping you finish more work in less time. And remember, AI tools thrive under strategic direction—use them to improve your work, not replace you.
