## Summary
Thinking of pitching for MozCon New York 2026? Learn how to create a standout speaker pitch, discover hot topics organizers love, and boost your chances of taking the stage.

## Extracted Body
MozCon New York is back for 2026, and the pitch form is now officially open!

We know many Community members are eager to pitch, but it can feel overwhelming if you're unsure what organizers are looking for.

The MozCon team receives hundreds of pitches, but only a few truly stand out.

In this post, I’ll explain how to write a compelling pitch, the topics we're excited about, and what happens after you hit submit.

Think of it like an elevator pitch. The committee reviews dozens of submissions daily, scanning for outstanding pitches. So, you need to make an immediate impact.

Make your title value-focused and easy to understand. Our core audience is SEO and digital marketers. Would your title excite them enough to buy tickets, grab their notebook, and take notes?

Use a bullet list to outline the main points you'll cover in your presentation. Ensure they align with the value you promised in your title and solve a real problem.

What challenges does your topic address? What fears or frustrations do SEOs and marketers face around it? Most importantly, what solution will leave them feeling confident and ready to act?

MozCon is all about empowering our audience to take action. We encourage speakers to make their talks practical, offering enough guidance for attendees to implement the tips they have learned.

Complete this sentence: By the end of this talk, the audience will know how to…

I encourage you to wear your empathy hat and think about the biggest problems digital marketers and SEOs face today.

AI search visibility is becoming just as important as visibility on traditional search engines. We'd love to hear from search specialists executing innovative strategies that have earned your clients or employers a significant increase in online visibility.

How are you using AI as a marketer to make more money for yourself or your clients? We love AI, and so does our audience. Show us the coolest things you’ve built with AI and how you use it to reach your SEO and marketing goals.

If you've conducted in-depth research that helps SEOs do their jobs better or get buy-in for execution, we'd love for you to share your research findings with our audience.

What’s the standout success you had in 2025? Is it replicable? Think about the strategy that drove that win and share how others can achieve similar results.

If you think of your skill set as an SEO or marketer, what’s one thing you do exceptionally well—a process you’ve perfected to the point that you’re almost protective of it because it consistently delivers massive results? That’s the talk we want to hear.

At the core, we're most interested in practical applications that help marketers and SEOs do their jobs better. We want people to return to work with something new they didn’t know before, something they learned from you and can apply immediately.

The deadline to submit a pitch is January 30, 2026. Due to the high volume of pitches we receive, we'll only contact speakers with whom we decide to move forward.

We’ll review pitches as they come in, so if you're interested in speaking at MozCon New York, it’s best to pitch now.

We can’t wait to hear your ideas. Submit your pitch today, and you could take the stage at MozCon 2026.
