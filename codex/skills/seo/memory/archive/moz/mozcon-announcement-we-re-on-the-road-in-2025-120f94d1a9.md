NOTE: MozCon 2025 pitches are now closed. Learn more about MozCon 2025 here.

MozCon is back in 2025, with a fresh look and feel, featuring two one-day events:

📍 London – Tuesday, August 12 📍 New York – Thursday, November 6

For 17 years, MozCon has been a pillar of the SEO community, where marketers connect, learn, and grow. Many of you have made lifelong friendships in Seattle, meeting colleagues, mentors, and friends who have shaped your careers. MozCon was never just an SEO conference. It’s a space where knowledge, connection, and community come together in powerful ways.

As the industry evolves, so does the way we gather. With teams working across different locations and more SEO events expanding globally, it’s time for MozCon to do the same. This new approach allows us to bring the MozCon experience to more people, creating meaningful connections in more places.

Many moons ago, we took the MozCon experience on the road, visiting SEOs in South Africa and London, among other places. We’re excited to reignite some of that energy as we reimagine what MozCon looks like so that even more of our Community gets the chance to experience what it’s all about.

While the format is changing, our commitment to this community remains as strong as ever. Moz was never just software. We’ve always been a community of professionals who care deeply about learning, sharing knowledge, and supporting each other’s success. That will never change.
