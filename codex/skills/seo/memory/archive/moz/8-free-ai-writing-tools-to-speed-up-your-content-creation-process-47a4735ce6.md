Moz Content Brief helps you move from research to writing faster by turning SERP data into a detailed brief. Instead of manually reviewing every ranking page, the tool analyzes top-ranking content and generates a brief that outlines what your article should cover.

As a marketer in a small team, here are a few use cases I love when using Moz AI Content Brief:

“I use Moz for SERP analysis by keyword, and it’s the first resource I go to when I need to assess my client's online presence, whether it's to set the benchmark/baseline or check for changes week over week. It is also super helpful to use the keyword suggestions for a website and see where the gaps are. I use it almost daily since it is user-friendly and easy to find what I’m looking for.”

Moz offers a 7-day free trial to test their suite of AI research tools, including content brief and AI visibility tracking. Paid plans start at $179/month for the medium option. Explore Moz Pro pricing here .

Get a data-backed content brief created in the click of a button! Available with your Moz Pro Free Trial.

ChatGPT has become the go-to AI writing assistant for marketers, with six out of ten relying on it to craft high-performing content. The versatility, ease of use, and ability to generate high-quality content across various formats make it an essential tool for writers , marketers, and content strategists.

As a product marketing manager at a crypto startup, here are a few use cases I love:

“I mainly use ChatGPT in the discovery phase and not for writing. I've used it to connect two topics — e.g., iconography and marketing…finding pain points for buyers and then brainstorming analogies for buyer problems.

ChatGPT offers a free plan with limited access to file uploads, advanced data analysis, web browsing, and image generation. Paid plans start at $20 monthly and include access to custom GPTs and improved performance. Get the complete list of their product features, offerings, and pricing here .

Developed by Anthropic, Claude.ai produces high-quality content while ensuring ethical AI behavior. It can process large amounts of text and maintain coherence over lengthy interactions, making it a powerful tool for writers, marketers, and content strategists.

“I wanted to illustrate a technically dry point to a non-technical person, so I reasoned with Claude.ai for a while and then got it to write its conclusions as a scene in an Aaron Sorkin screenplay. It was so good, clear, and emotionally compelling that I left it 99% untouched. It saved me hours and was my best-received email of the year.”

Claude offers a free plan alongside paid options starting at $20 per month. Explore Claude’s pricing and plans in detail here , or sign up for a free Claude account here .

Microsoft Copilot is an AI assistant integrated into Microsoft 365 applications. Whether drafting documents, analyzing data, or managing emails, Copilot reduces manual effort and improves efficiency.

“Copilot researches a subject with more specific and targeted results than a search engine. It's easy to use, integrated with Office 365 tools, and can access all internal documents.”

Copilot has a free version with limited usage capabilities and a paid version starting at $20 per user/month. You can try Copilot here .

Writesonic is a marketing powerhouse that offers real-time web research, competitor analysis , and automated optimization.

“I've been using Writesonic for a while now, and I can confidently say it has completely transformed how I approach content creation. As someone who juggles multiple projects, Writesonic has made me more efficient by speeding up my workflow without compromising quality.

Writesonic offers a free trial with 25 one-time credits. The Individual plan starts at $16 per month. You can sign up for Writesonic’s free trial here.

Wordtune is an AI writing assistant that offers content rephrasing suggestions, tone adjustments, and sentence restructuring. Whether you're polishing an article, improving clarity in emails, or fine-tuning social media copy, Wordtune makes the editing process faster and more efficient.

“I like that it allows me to write what I am trying to communicate and then use it to help me figure out the best way to phrase it.”

Wordtune offers a free plan with limited usage and an advanced plan starting at $6.99/month. You can explore their Teams plans here .

Copy.ai is a versatile AI writing tool designed to streamline content creation with pre-built templates, brand voice customization, and collaborative workflows.

“From a sales perspective, I can orchestrate workflows to enrich data on prospects, accounts, and more, enabling us to personalize automated inbound emails. We can also equip our SDRs with highly specific research relevant to our market, so they don't have to start from scratch when researching new accounts.”

You can sign up for a ‘Free Forever’ Copy.ai plan here , or you can try the starter plan for $49/month.

Perplexity AI is an intelligent research assistant that delivers accurate results with cited sources, prompts, and follow-up questions. It remembers past interactions for a more intuitive experience, making it a powerful tool for writers, marketers, and researchers looking to streamline content creation.

“I use Perplexity to analyze what other articles are being cited for a specific question around a topic. I then follow some best practices to get my articles featured in the cited resources. This way, I have increased my referral traffic from Perplexity by 67% over the past couple of months.”

Perplexity offers a free plan alongside its Pro plan ($20/month), including 300 Pro searches per month, unlimited free daily searches, and a choice of advanced AI models. You can sign up for a new account on a free plan here .

AI writing tools can improve productivity and writing output but are not a replacement for creativity. Start with a clear brief, review outputs for accuracy, and add your unique insights to personalize the content.
