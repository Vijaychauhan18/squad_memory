# How to Build a Strong Brand Moat With SEO

Source: Moz Blog
Homepage: https://moz.com/blog
Original URL: https://moz.com/blog/build-a-strong-brand-moat
Published: 2024-06-11
Strength: SEO education, beginner-to-advanced resources, brand/entity SEO, whiteboard-style explainers

## Summary
When you establish a distinct and powerful brand identity, your business can protect itself from the competition in much the same way a moat protects a castle. Learn how to build a strong brand moat with SEO in this blog post.

## Extracted Body
The connection between building your brand and SEO is much stronger than many realize. We know that brand signals are important to Google, as suspected by many, and confirmed via the Google documentation leak in late May.

At its core, increasing brand visibility can be seen as a fundamental aspect of SEO.

By integrating brand-focused strategies into your SEO efforts , you're not just aiming for immediate traffic boosts but making your brand so distinct and well-regarded that it stands out from competitors.

It’s like the groundwork for sustainable long-term growth – equivalent to a moat protecting your castle.

This approach enhances conversion rates by making your brand more recognizable to potential customers and significantly improves the overall customer experience.

Keep reading to learn how building a moat with SEO can significantly drive business impact and lay a solid foundation for long-term brand recognition and visibility.

SEO isn't just about getting to the top of search engine results; it's about cementing your brand's presence in customers' minds.

Establishing a 'brand moat' through SEO is essential for business defense and differentiation.

When people search for anything related to what you offer and consistently find your brand among the top results, they start to see you as the go-to authority.

Hence, it’s important to have a well-executed SEO strategy that extends beyond visibility and translates into real business growth like:

1. Higher conversion rates: When people trust your brand, they're more likely to buy from you. SEO helps by ensuring your brand shows up where and when it matters, leading to more conversions .

2. Customer loyalty: A strong brand moat means customers keep coming back. SEO helps maintain this loyalty by keeping you visible and relevant.

3. Long-term growth : SEO isn’t a one-and-done deal. It requires constant updating and adjusting, but the payoff is sustainable and builds on itself over time.

When you focus on SEO and building a strong brand, you're doing more than just trying to get clicks or make a quick sale.

Brands like Hotjar have leveraged SEO to generate a 47% increase in traffic and a 20% increase in customers , proving how SEO can help drive business value.

Creating a strong brand moat for B2B brands requires more than publishing high-quality content.

The real challenge lies in establishing the trust and visibility of your brand.

To stand out in such a competitive environment, you need to take a strategic approach to content creation and SEO.

This involves prioritizing strategies that not only build authority but also drive traffic and revenue.

Competing with A-players is less daunting when you put focus on specific, relevant topics rather than casting a wide net with your content strategy.

Let’s take a look at the key elements of a strong brand moat.

Imagine you're in the superstore to buy a new electric toothbrush but unsure which model to choose. It's unlikely you'll be swayed solely by the manufacturer's claims. Instead, you're likely to seek opinions from trusted third-party publications and peers.

This is where dominating Search Engine Results Pages (SERPs) becomes crucial for your brand. That’s how achieving SERP dominance solidifies your Brand's Authority TM in the industry, making it a Moat that allows brands to maintain and sustain organic results.

However, your wrong approach to SEO and brand building can dilute the brand's narrative and slow the building of authority.

So, the key to accelerating growth and attracting quality search traffic lies in telling a cohesive, compelling story through your content — one chapter at a time and building a strong SERP presence.

Take MasterClass , for example, a brand renowned for its heavy investment in advertising across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.

In 2019 alone, Business Insider highlighted a staggering $6.5 million expenditure on ads , a figure that surged in the following months as demand for its offerings spiked. Despite its vast advertising budget, the primary traffic source to MasterClass's website and application is organic search.

As you can see in the Google search results, Masterclass consistently secures the top spot for a broad spectrum of keywords, from highly specific terms to those with expansive monthly search volumes.

This achievement underscores a pivotal strategy: satisfying search intent with the appropriate information that they are looking for.

More than merely targeting keywords with significant search volume, MasterClass's success is attributed to crafting content that aligns with what users search for.

By aligning content with user intent and ensuring consistency in quality across all touchpoints, brands can leverage SERP dominance to influence purchasing decisions and foster long-term customer loyalty.

To dominate the SERP, establishing topical authority with high-value content is essential.

Topical authority is about building authority around a topic that aligns closely with your industry, product, and value proposition.

You achieve this status by consistently providing valuable, expert content that addresses the needs and answers the questions of your audience.

If your goal is to generate traffic and turn SEO into a revenue-generating channel, building topical authority in your space is the best way to go about it.

Once you’ve built trust, momentum takes over, and people begin to pay attention to everything you publish.

This narrow focus is far more effective than solely aiming for a high Domain Authority , especially for startups and businesses looking to make a tangible impact in competitive markets.

Dominating the SERP with topical authority accelerates your organic growth and establishes a protective moat around your brand.

Ultimately, your business can not only attract but also retain a loyal audience, turning SEO efforts into a revenue-generating channel.

When brand excellence is combined with content excellence, the competitive advantage and moat created around your business can significantly deter competitors.

You’ve learned about the essential elements of an SEO moat. Now for the meaty part: here are six practical tactics to build topical authority.

Topic clusters are the backbone of building topical authority. These are groups of semantically related articles that interlink, covering a core topic and its subtopics extensively.

At the heart of a topic cluster is usually a pillar page , which serves as the hub from which cluster pages branch out.

By thoroughly covering each sub-niche, your site gains more authority in the overall niche, which, in turn, fortifies your digital moat.

However, this raises the question: How many pages on a specific topic does a website need to be considered an authority?

The answer isn't straightforward and varies depending on the scope of the topic.

For broad, highly competitive topics, achieving comprehensive coverage — and thereby, SERP dominance — might necessitate the creation of hundreds of pages.

This is because the more content you have on a topic, the more likely search engines are to recognize your site as a leading source of information.
