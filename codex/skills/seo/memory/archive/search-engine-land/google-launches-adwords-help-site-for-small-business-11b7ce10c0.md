## Extracted Body
Google has announced a new website aimed at offering tips and advice for small business owners that use AdWords. It’s called the AdWords Small Business Center .

It offers tips in several aspects of the AdWords system — like “Write great ads” and “Adjust bids and budgets” — and the content is divided into Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced material. There are also links to the official AdWords blog and a discussion forum just for small business owners. Haven’t really dug into it yet myself, but it looks pretty helpful.
