## Extracted Body
The full length trailer for the social saga based on Facebook debuted on JT’s official blog and Yahoo Movies earlier today:

The full length movie is scheduled to be released in early October, bringing the story of Facebook’s rise to fame to the big screen. From the trailer, it looks like we’ll see quite a bit of Boston style backstabbing from its early days of development at Harvard (was that the same bar from Good Will Hunting I spotted in the clip?) and the courtroom drama ensuing from lawsuits over security, privacy and copyright infringement.

The movie stars Justin Timberlake as Napster’s founder Sean Parker, who gives advice to the young upstart, with Mark Zuckerberg portrayed by Jesse Eisenberg (of Zombieland fame), Parks and Recreation’s Rashida Jones also co-stars. Full post-production details can be found at IMDB . The official movie website is: www.thesocialnetwork-movie.com.

Viewing party at SMX NYC in October, anyone? Get your socially salted popcorn ready…
