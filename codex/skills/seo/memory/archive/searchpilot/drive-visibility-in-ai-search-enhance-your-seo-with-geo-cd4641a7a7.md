## Extracted Body
Your customers still search before they buy. But they’re turning to AI and LLMs first. SearchPilot reveals how AI prioritizes search results and what shoppers actually click by running Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) A/B tests on your PDPs and PLPs.

Achieve greater visibility in AI overviews. Expand your reach by appearing in more search queries. Determine key revenue drivers. Prepare for the next trillion searches.

SEO tests measure the impact of website changes on organic traffic, search rankings, and conversions. GEO focuses on whether your buyers reach your site after it is featured in AI-generated answers and overviews.

Our scientific approach to testing remains the gold standard. We use control and variant page groups to measure impact. You still need statistical rigour to know what truly works.

Your most important pages are your Product Detail Pages (PDPs) and Product Listing Pages (PLPs). They remain a primary focus for both SEO and GEO optimization.

Your testing focus shifts from top-of-funnel visibility to bottom-of-funnel influence. You're no longer testing against a single search result, but an entire AI-driven research journey.

LLMs have two different knowledge sources: static information they learned from training data and ‘live’ knowledge from searches and specific web pages (also called Retrieval Augmented Generation or ‘RAG’) . You can’t easily change AI’s static information. But with GEO A/B testing, you can impact RAG.

Make your product and category pages the best source for an AI model as it guides users towards making a purchase.

Increase your coverage across fan-out queries performed by the AI by ranking for more keywords, terms and queries.

Experiment to see which content or format appears most attractive in the search results returned to the AI or LLM.
