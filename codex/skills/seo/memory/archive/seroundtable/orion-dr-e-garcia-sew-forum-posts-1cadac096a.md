I normally do not pick out individual members at forums but one such member deserves a special mentioning. This member, now an SEW Forum Moderator, goes by the user name Orion , but is formally known as Dr. E. Garcia . SEW Forums, in my opinion, is lucky to have such a unique individual participating daily in the forum.

Lets just take a look at a selected number of Dr. Garcia's threads : Orion's first post named Keywords Co-occurrence and Semantic Connectivity has over 23,900 thread views! He followed up the previous thread with an other outstanding thread named Term Vector Theory and Keyword Weights . and On-Topic Analysis also Local Context Analysis and Block Analysis 101 really started a rumble of future thinking in the SEM community his most recent thread, Temporal Link Analysis is going to be one to watch.

All of these posts have impacted my views on my understanding of search technology. This one individual deserves or thanks.
