I would like to welcome everyone to the first RustyBrick Weblog.

There are several reasons that I started this Weblog, including; (1) Everyone else is doing it! :), (2) A place to share my thoughts with others, if they want to listen, (3) SEO reasons ;), (4) Personal rambling reasons, (5) Most important reason - to create a valuable and informative SEO resource comprised of some of the most talented SEOs, coders, site owners and searchers on the Web.

The goal is to get a well rounded view of search engine optimization and how it affects SEOs, Programmers and Designers, site owners, and the average search engine user.To expant on # 5, i.e. to create a valuable and informative SEO resource comprised of some of the most talented SEOs, coders and searchers on the Web.

I will be asking fellow SEOs from the industry that I know are experienced and knowledgable in the industry to become an author. In additon, I will ask some programmers and designers that do not consider SEO as their primary service offerings to join. Finally I will be asking some average Joe searchers to share their thoughts with the readers.

The goal is to get a well rounded view of search engine optimization and how it affects SEOs, Programmers and Designers, site owners, and the average search engine user.
