## Extracted Body
I assume this can be released, it is publicly available at Search Guild Forum . They call it a "forum for SEO forum mods and admins", and the name of the forum is Forum Speak . Basically, it is a private forum for moderators and administrators of SEM forums to come and discuss moderating and administrating issues at the collective forums.

As mentioned a Search Guild, here is the purpose as stated by the forums creator, Chris Ridings. We've been thinking about seo forums working collaboratively to help each other out. We figure that we could give the mods and admins of other forums useful information and they could possibly notify us about a thing or too. In short we've been thinking "how can the mods and admins of the different forums discuss issues in order to resolve issues that apply to all the forums". Say "John Smith" is a forum spamming robot, shouldn't there be somewhere mods can warn the mods of other forums?

If you are an admin or mod at a forum, then check out the thread for more details.
