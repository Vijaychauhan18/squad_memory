Our event series, Search Central Live, is coming to Toronto, Canada on April 21, 2026 for the first time! The event series has been a great way for communities to connect with each other and Googlers to learn more about the web ecosystem and how Google Search works and exchange ideas.

The event will have presenters from the Google Search Relations and other Google teams. The content will be presented in English. Topics include:

Besides presentations and a Q&A session, there will be opportunities to meet Googlers and other attendees.

This event is aimed at website owners, publishers, professionals working with news organizations, digital marketers, web developers, SEO professionals, or folks otherwise interested in websites in Search. So if you're interested in these topics, work with websites and want to join us, apply for a seat to join us there!

The event won't be recorded or live streamed, that's why we call it live!

Unfortunately, we can't accommodate everyone. Sign up until April 14th. We'll send invitations by April 16th to accepted attendees, you'll need that email to enter the event. The event will take place on April 21, between 9:00 and 14:00 in Toronto (exact address will follow with the invitation).
