### What is an AGaaS company?

Simple Explanation: An AGaaS company delivers autonomous AI agents as a service, rather than traditional software tools.

- SaaS (what we have today): You subscribe to software/apps. You (a human) log in and do the work using the tool.
- AGaaS (the next evolution): You subscribe to AI agents that do the work for you autonomously – they plan, reason, use tools, execute multi-step tasks, and deliver outcomes.

- Autonomy : Agents act independently (with minimal human oversight).
- Outcome-focused : You pay for results/tasks completed, not just seats or features.
- Agentic AI : Agents can handle complex workflows (e.g., full sales processes, customer support, data analysis) across systems.
- Enabled by open stacks : Huang highlighted technologies like OpenClaw (compared to Linux/Kubernetes), making this scalable.

In short, AGaaS shifts software from being a tool humans use to intelligence that works on your behalf .
