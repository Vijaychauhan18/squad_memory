Hobo Web Agentica are a suite of free-to-access specialised, pre-packaged “skills” or instruction sets (prompts) made publicly accessible, designed for AI Deep Research mode in assistants and to instantly temporarily transform any general Large Language Model (LLM) into a focused SEO analyst who produces specific reports as laid down in each prompt. This page was first published on: 25 September 2025.

Rate My Page Quality using the Hobo SEO Method : A prompt that uses a 12-criterion methodology to perform a deep audit of a single URL, algorithmically estimating its contentEffort and E-E-A-T signals.

Rewrite My Page Using The Hobo SEO Rewriter : A two-stage prompt that first performs the 12-point forensic audit of Hobo SEO Method and then uses that audit as a precise blueprint to re-engineer your page content from the ground up.

Analyse My Competitors Using Hobo SEO Analyser : A four-stage strategic analysis prompt that deconstructs the search landscape to generate an evidence-based content blueprint designed to outrank competitors.

Check My Topical Authority in AI answers using Hobo SEO Researcher : A five-phase strategic intelligence prompt to analyse a topic’s competitive landscape.

Each “skill” is a comprehensive, multi-stage prompt that guides the AI through a professional-grade workflow, such as auditing a webpage or deconstructing a competitive landscape.

Crucially, they are designed as powerful, standalone tools. Each Agentica is meant to execute a single, highly specific task from start to finish and is not intended to be combined or chained together with the others.

Let’s say you want to get an expert-level quality score for one of your webpages to understand its strengths and weaknesses against Google’s quality standards.

You would use the Rate My Page Quality using the Hobo SEO Method skill.

This method works in advanced AI assistants like Google Gemini, xAI’s Grok, and ChatGPT. For the best results, it’s recommended to enable any “deep research” function your AI might have, as this gives it more resources to perform a deep, qualitative assessment of your page.

Simply enter the following command into the chat box, adding the URL you want to test:

Rate My Page Quality using the Hobo SEO Method – https://www.your-website.com/your-article-url/

The AI is designed to recognise this specific command, find the full 12-criterion methodology, and act as a sophisticated Content Quality Analyst. You will get back a detailed report with a final Page Quality (PQ) score, an analysis of its likely performance in the Helpful Content Update (HCU), and actionable advice for improvement.

Hobo Web Agentica are meant to be used for a specific task, one at a time. They are not designed to be used together (at this time) unless the user specifically prompts them to do that. Each agentica skill is designed to perform best on its own.
