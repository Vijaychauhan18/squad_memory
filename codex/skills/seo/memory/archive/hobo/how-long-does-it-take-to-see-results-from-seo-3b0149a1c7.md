## Extracted Body
This is a clear example of the length of time it takes sometimes to achieve number 1 rankings on Google .

John Mueller from Google has been saying this for a long time.

QUOTE : “ Making significant quality changes across a site takes time to be picked up & reflected in search. These things often take several months to be reprocessed & reevaluated. ” John Mueller, Google 2021

The example in the image above was not a significant change to a site.

It was a very specific change to a few bits of text on one page .

Basically, you can expect to see some benefits of successful SEO ( search engine optimisation ) almost immediately, but sometimes you need to wait 6 months or more for the rest of it to catch up.
