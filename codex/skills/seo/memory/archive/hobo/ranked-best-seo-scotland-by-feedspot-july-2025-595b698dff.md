Scottish SEO features in top UK Rankings, with Hobo voted #1

30/07/2025 – GREENOCK, INVERCLYDE, SCOTLAND, UK – The 2025 Feedspot rankings for the “ 60 Best UK SEO Blogs ” have been released, revealing an incredible result for Scotland’s digital marketing scene.

Two of the top three spots have been awarded to Scottish-based blogs , cementing the nation’s position as a player in the UK’s search industry.

The Hobo SEO blog, by Shaun Anderson , has taken the #1 position , with Glasgow’s Craig Campbell SEO securing the #3 spot.

This remarkable achievement showcases the depth of expertise and innovation happening right here in Scotland.

To have two Scottish blogs in the top three of a comprehensive UK-wide list, which ranks thousands of sites on authority, freshness, and relevance, is a powerful statement. It highlights that the epicentre of cutting-edge SEO thinking isn’t confined to London.

Shaun Anderson, founder of Hobo, commented on the news: “We’re proud not just to be back at number one, but to share the top tier with a fellow Scot. It’s a massive win for the Scottish SEO community and proves that world-class expertise is thriving right here. For Scotland to have two of the top three blogs in the UK is a well-deserved recognition of the talent based here.”

The success story doesn’t end at the top. In total, five Scottish blogs were featured in the top 60, demonstrating a nationwide strength in the digital sector.

Congratulations are also due to Alba SEO Services, Platonik, and iCONQUER for their inclusion in this prestigious list.
