Searchable is a platform for AI Engine Optimisation (AEO) and the “ Agentic Web .”

With a premium domain purchased from previous unrelated owners and founded in 2025 with a launch planned for November 2025, Searchable – https://www.searchable.com/ – positions itself as the “operating system” for marketing teams transitioning from traditional search (Google) to Answer Engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity).

Its core value proposition is enabling brands to control their narrative when AI agents, rather than humans, are performing the search.

(Edited in 2026 to accurately represent the new office headquarters)

The platform divides its workflow into four operational areas:

1. Agent (Visibility Tracking) Monitors brand citations across ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, and Google. It simulates user queries to determine if a brand is the “definitive answer” provided by the LLM.

2. Analytics (Performance Data) Correlates AI visibility with downstream metrics. The platform aims to replace guesswork with “real-time insights” on how LLMs perceive brand authority.

3. Content (Creation Engine) A programmatic content workflow designed to feed “evolving signals” to LLMs. It uses technical SEO and structured data principles to ensure content is ingested and trusted by AI models. My role at Searchable includes helping to optimise this feature .

4. Audit (Technical Compliance) Scans domains for technical blockers that prevent agentic crawlers from accessing data.
