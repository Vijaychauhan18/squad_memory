## Extracted Body
Your brand ranks on the first page of Google. Your domain authority is solid. Yet when a prospect asks Perplexity, “What are the best solutions for [your category]?” , your company doesn’t appear in the cited sources. Competitors you outrank in traditional search get mentioned. You don’t.

This disconnect drives demand for Perplexity SEO, also called GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) or AEO (Answer Engine Optimization), which focuses on making brands eligible, extractable, and credible enough to be cited in AI-generated answers. The challenge: most agencies claiming Perplexity expertise are rebranding existing SEO services without understanding how AI answer engines evaluate and cite sources differently than traditional search.

“It’s not just about what Perplexity can do – it’s about what it doesn’t do. No sponsored results shoved in your face. No SEO-laden blogs packed with ads. No digging through endless links for tiny nuggets of info. It just gives you the answers – clean, direct, and without the BS.”

This guide ranks the top 10 agencies based on technical eligibility depth, measurement infrastructure, experiment-driven methodology, platform access insight, competitive gap analysis, and pricing transparency.
