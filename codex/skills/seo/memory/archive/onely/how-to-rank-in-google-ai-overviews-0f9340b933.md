Your domain authority improved slightly. Yet organic traffic dropped 40% year-over-year.

This disconnect has a clear explanation: 60.32% of U.S. search queries now trigger AI Overviews. When they appear, organic CTR drops from 1.76% to 0.61% – a 65% decline .

The rules changed in 11 months. AI Overview prevalence grew 670% from January to November 2025. Your rankings held. User behavior shifted underneath them.

This isn’t a gradual trend you can address in next year’s planning. Competitors appearing in AI responses for your category queries are capturing consideration before prospects ever reach your site.

The impact is being felt across industries. As one SEO consultant shared on r/SEO :

“I am the SEO consultant for a large online course brand. Not going to say who, but since AI overviews there traffic has dropped 65% YoY while some how rankings are at an all time high. IMO if your site is in the in the knowledge selling Buisness AI is going to, if it hasn’t already, taken you out.”

AI Overviews don’t select sources randomly. Research reveals specific signals that predict citation likelihood.

92.36% of AI Overview citations come from domains ranking in Google’s top 10, according to Single Grain . A seoClarity study found 99% correlation between high organic rankings and AI Overview links.

Your existing SEO investment isn’t wasted – it’s foundational. But ranking alone isn’t sufficient. GetPassionfruit found that ranking #1 gives only a 33.07% chance of appearing in AI Overviews. 26% of brands have zero AI mentions regardless of their rankings.

This is the counterintuitive finding that changes resource allocation: Ahrefs research shows brand mentions correlate at 0.664 with AI visibility, versus 0.218 for backlinks.

Brands in the top 25% for web mentions earn 10x more AI citations than the next quartile. According to AIrops , brands with both mentions and citations are 40% more likely to resurface in multiple AI responses.

What this means for budget: A press mention generating 15 unlinked brand mentions may drive more AI visibility than 15 DA40 backlinks.

SE Ranking research found that when AI Overviews and featured snippets both appear, 61.79% share the same source. Featured snippet optimization transfers directly to AI visibility.

Content structured to win featured snippets – direct answers, clear lists, concise definitions – performs well for AI extraction.

AI systems extract content in approximately 800-token chunks, according to Strapi . Average AI Overview summaries run 169 words with 7.2 links.

The Google Developers Blog confirms: content should use “chunk-level retrieval with fact-rich, concise passages that can stand alone.”

Your 2,500-word pillar page optimized for dwell time may underperform a 1,200-word page with clear sections, each leading with a direct answer.

Ahrefs found the top 5 most-cited domains are Wikipedia, YouTube, Google Blog, Reddit, and Google.com. SellersCommerce reports Reddit appears in 68% of AI Overview results.

Reddit citations increased 450% between March and June 2025. User-generated content now comprises 21.74% of all AI Overview citations. AI systems favor authentic, community-validated information over polished brand content.

SevenAtoms found 88% of AI answers target informational, long-tail queries. WordStream reports AI Overviews have only a 10% chance of appearing for commercial or transactional keywords.

Optimizing product pages for AI Overviews wastes resources. Focus GEO efforts on informational content; keep traditional SEO for transactional pages.
