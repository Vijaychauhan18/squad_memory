Seems like everyone is starting to take SEO seriously now, except Seth Godin! Like many other large ecommerce systems, sites, and others who may have viewed search engine optimization as snake oil in the past, eBay is now searching for an SEO specialist on HotJobs! Associate Manager, Internet Marketing Natural Search at eBay Inc. – Yahoo! HotJobs

The eBay Natural Search Initiative is a cross-functional effort to allow eBay pages to rank well in natural search engines. The Internet Marketing team is looking for a motivated individual to manage the companys natural search projects while developing the overall natural search strategy. As a key member of the team running one of the industry’s largest online advertising programs, you will play a vital role in driving the growth of eBay’s revenue and profitability.

The role will be responsible for executing projects utilizing a broad set of skills including project management, problem solving, data analysis and communication. Key responsibilities:

Work with Product Management to develop new natural search projects and define requirements Work with many cross-functional teams to execute on projects, including Category Management, Product Management, Product Development, Marketing, Design, Operations, Architecture Prepareand present senior management communications concerning key natural search issues and initiatives Analyze, test, and execute on long-term strategies to enhance natural search effectiveness Continually optimize natural search performance through analysis of data, as well as identification, application, and communication of best practices

The ideal candidate will have experience similar to that gainedat a world class consulting firm or corporate strategy group. Strong problem solving and analytical skills, strategic thought, and comfort with operating in a dynamic and fast-paced workplace are musts.

3-4 years of experience in consulting, strategic planning, banking, e-commerce, and/or marketing Bachelor’s degree required Excel at project management and multi-tasking in an environment of rapidly shifting priorities The successful candidate will be highly organized, have excellent analytical and problem solving skills Strong communications skills, both written and verbal Ability to interact with all relevant levels of management Passion for and understanding of eBay considered a strong plus Proficient in Excel, Access and PowerPoint Teamwork and enthusiasm a must Good natured senseof humor
