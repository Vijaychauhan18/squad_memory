Step no. 1 Register a .com, a .biz, a .cc or a .ca domain name (.ca are reserved for Canadian companies only). There are also many additional options that can be added to any e-commerce registered domain name at any time such as the ability to:

Park the domain with a “Coming Soon” page Redirect the domain name to another of your choice Redirect to a subdirectory of another domain name Start using your new domain name email address Add relevant body text & keywords to your pages
