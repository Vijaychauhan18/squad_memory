Find.com, a business search engine (www.find.com), today announced that it has entered into a strategic partnership with MarketResearch.com Inc., the leading online source for global market intelligence reports. Under the terms of the strategic partnership, MarketResearch.com, which offers more than 80,000 reports from over 350 research firms and consultancies worldwide, has become an on-line content partner for Find.com. The partnership with MarketResearch.com enhances the precise and reliable business-related information that Find.com provides by expanding the collection of premium content that is available as part of specific search results.

“Our commitment to make the most respected and trusted information available to researchers either on a pay-per-document or free basis is greatly enhanced by the addition of MarketResearch.com to our roster of content providers,” stated Chris Travers, CEO of Find.com. “The accessibility of this type of reliable content is the primary reason business professionals have come to trust Find.com.”

Find.com was founded in June 2004 as a partnership of FIND/SVP Inc., Empire Media and TripleHop Technologies. Find.com provides a solution for business professionals in need of precise and reliable business-related information.

Today’s annouced agreement makes available a vast majority of MarketResearch.com’s syndicated research reports – covering global and regional industries, products and companies – to Find.com. Visitors will be able to view the titles, abstracts and table of contents for MarketResearch.com reports via Find.com. Complete reports from MarketResearch.com are available on a pay-per-document basis.

“This partnership furthers our twin goals of helping business information users find the right information, and exposing our information providers to new markets and customers,” said Robert Granader, CEO of MarketResearch.com. “Our longstanding relationship with FIND/SVP and our position as the leading providers of market research information on the web make this relationship a natural fit.”
