Moreover Technologies, a provider of syndicated news and online information, this week announced a new service that enables bloggers to significantly expand the distribution of their content by automatically notifying Moreover the instant new content is published. The Moreover Ping Server ensures rapid inclusion into the vast Moreover online news and current awareness distribution network, made up of leading destination portals, corporations and consumers.

Configuring Weblog authoring software to automatically call the Moreover Ping Server provides several key advantages:

* Increased content distribution – Direct access to the Moreover distribution network, made up of leading destination portals and corporate clients, enables bloggers to drive more site traffic and maximize any monetization. * Reduced time from publishing to mass distribution – By notifying Moreover when new content is published, content can appear on the network within 2 to 30 minutes. * Greater control over content collection – Control when and how new content notifications occur, by site or by individual posts.

“We are pleased to give publishers control over the speed with which they notify Moreover of new content,” said Jim Pitkow, Chief Executive Officer and President of Moreover Technologies. “By using our Ping Server, publishers can ensure that their content is immediately available across Moreover’s unparalleled distribution network.”
