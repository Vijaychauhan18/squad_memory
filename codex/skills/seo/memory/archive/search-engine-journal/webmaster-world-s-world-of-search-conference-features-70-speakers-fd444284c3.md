Webmaster World’s World of Search Conference Features 70 Speakers

The WebmasterWorld of Search Conference scheduled for November 16-18 in Las Vegas has published a final speaker roster and announced over 24 sessions with more than 70 industry-leading speakers. The line up features speakers from such companies as Google, Yahoo, Kanoodle, Ask Jeeves, Ebay, Overture, Mirago, InfoSearch Media, Quigo, FindWhat, IBM, Urchin, GenieKnows, and ClickTracks. The full list of speakers and sponsors can be found at http://www.webmasterworld.com/conference, the conference website.

The event will feature a Keynote Speech from Noel McMichael, CEO of Marketleap, and a special session hosted by Google. “There are three Super Sessions this time, including ‘Webmaster & Search Engines’, ‘Search Engine Optimization History & Theory’, and ‘Running with the Big Dogs/Are You Master of Your Domain?'” said Brett Tabke, WebmasterWorld’s CEO. “This is by far our biggest event to date. The conference fills in the old Comdex week in Las Vegas.”

The conference is perfect for webmasters and those involved in the search engine industry, the online advertising industry, affiliate marketing, or any business with an Internet presence. “Not only will you get to hear the latest from the industry leaders, you’ll get the chance to meet them, too.” commented Tabke.
