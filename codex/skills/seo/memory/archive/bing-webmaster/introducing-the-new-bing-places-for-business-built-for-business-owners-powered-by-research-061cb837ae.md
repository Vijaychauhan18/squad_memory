Today, we are excited to announce the launch of the new Bing Places for Business experience —an evolution shaped by deep user research, thoughtful design, and a commitment to help business owners thrive in local search. Bing Places for Business is a free online platform where businesses can create and manage their listings to appear in Bing search results and Bing Maps, making them more visible to potential customers. Listening First: What Business Owners Told Us Before we wrote a single line of code, we spent months listening to business owners about their challenges and goals. We took these insights to heart and rebuilt the experience from the ground up. A Unified Experience Under Bing.com One of the most significant changes is the migration of the entire business owner’s experience into the Bing.com domain. This move simplifies access, improves trust, and aligns Bing Places with the broader Bing ecosystem. Whether you're claiming a listing, updating business hours, or uploading photos, everything now lives in one cohesive space. As part of this transition, the domain will change from www.bingplaces.com to www.bing.com/forbusiness , making it easier for business owners to find and manage their listings directly within the Bing experience. The new experience is now live and will continue adding further features in the following months to ensure a complete and polished rollout. Our current users and their business listings will be automatically migrated to the new account and current users will be redirected to this brand-new experience when they login with their Bing Places for Business credentials.

Smarter Imports, Smoother Management We’ve made major improvements to how business owners import listings from Google. The new import flow is faster, more reliable, and better at preserving key attributes like business name, hours, and contact details. Once imported, managing listings is easier than ever—with intuitive dashboards, bulk editing tools, and real-time status updates.
