Many years ago, Joost de Valk launched version 1.0 of a plugin the world would know as the number one WordPress SEO plugin: Yoast SEO. Today, we’re celebrating the sweet sixteen of Yoast SEO with the release of Yoast SEO 16.0. Of course, we’re not letting this go unnoticed!

While the Yoast SEO 16.0 itself isn’t a huge release, as we’ve mainly added some fixes and enhancements, but we still wanted to take this occasion to celebrate reaching the big 16. This week, we’ll tell the story of Yoast SEO and how it came to be from the people who worked on it closely.

We’re kicking things off with Marieke van de Rakt , our CEO, who’ll uncover sixteen reasons to love Yoast SEO . Tomorrow, the founder of Yoast and the inventor/original developer of Yoast SEO, Joost de Valk , will discuss the impact the WordPress SEO plugin had over the years in an interview . And, of course, he’ll give us an insight into how he sees Yoast SEO grow up to be 18! Plus, we’ve collected a visual history of Yoast SEO on a brand-new feature timeline page .

We’ve always treated Yoast SEO Premium as a separate plugin from Yoast SEO. While that made sense in the past, we’ve concluded that providing it as an add-on to Yoast SEO is a much better proposition. For one, it makes it easier to maintain because all products now share the same codebase. But, more importantly, it’ll help us get ready to offer you a much more seamless experience buying and installing Yoast SEO Premium in the future.

Conceptually, as a free user, you will be able to upgrade to Yoast SEO Premium automatically (or as frictionless as possible), making the Premium plugin an “add-on” for the free edition. In other words, Yoast SEO Premium is now a companion to the free version, and you need both plugins to work.

With Yoast SEO 16.0, we’re out to celebrate! In our sweet sixteen release, we had several fixes and improvements. Also, we made Yoast SEO Premium an add-on to Yoast SEO, making it much easier to maintain and improve the installation flow for new customers.
