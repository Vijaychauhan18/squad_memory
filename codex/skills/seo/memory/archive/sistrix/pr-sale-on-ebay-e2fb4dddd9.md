## Extracted Body
At the moment, you can marvel at an extremely brazen case of a sham-pagerank on ebay.de . The site for sale did not get the PageRank by itself but by being classified as a yahoo.com synonym by Google ( Google cache-version ). For all of you who always wanted a PR10 site: 1. Register your domain and put a few links on it to get it into the index. 2. Redirect the DNS-server to yahoo.com 3. Wait for the next Google PR-update and voila, you’re done.
