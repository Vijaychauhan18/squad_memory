# Ask Jeeves buys Excite Europe

## Summary
Ask Jeeves is buying the Excite Italia B.V, which means Excite Europe, from Tiscali. According to Tiscali, the price was 6,1 million Euros. The new owner is planning on consolidating Excite Europe with the US-branch of Excite, which they bought back in March of 2004.
