## Summary
According to the Wirtschaftswoche, T-Online, after Google, Yahoo and Microsoft, now wants to offer their own desktop-searchengine. They are planning on debuting their 2.5 MB software at the CeBIT. The search is based on a modified version of the Copernic software.
