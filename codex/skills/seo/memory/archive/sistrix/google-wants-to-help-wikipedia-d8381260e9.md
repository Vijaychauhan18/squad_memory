## Summary
The Wikimedia-foundation announced that Google has offered to host part of the Wikipedia-project. They are appropriating a number of Dual-Xeon-Servers as well as unlimited bandwidth. This offer is explicitly not linked to an inclusion of AdSense banners on Wikipedia.org.
