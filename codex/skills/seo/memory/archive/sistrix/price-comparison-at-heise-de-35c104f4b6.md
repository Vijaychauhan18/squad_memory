## Extracted Body
Now, heise.de also has its own price-comparison. The Site for the heise-publishing-house, publishes the three magazines c’t, iX and Technology Review, offers an online price-comparison in cooperation with the mentasys GmbH at www.heise.de/preisvergleich/ . According to heise, they are comparing the prices of about 100 merchants. Thanks to a high linkpopularity that heise.de has been amassing for years, it can be assumed that the searchengines will list the price-comparison site rather well.
