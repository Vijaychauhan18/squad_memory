Google just opened its e-mail service GMail up for everyone in the USA. Until now, you needed an invitation of a GMail-user to get your hands on an account. To sign up you will now need to enter a cell-phone number where the confirmation SMS will be send. This is to safeguard GMail from being abused by spammers.
