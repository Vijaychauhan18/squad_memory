## Extracted Body
According to a study by Nielsen//Netratings , EBay had more than 22 Million European visitors from Great Britain, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Sweden, the Netherlands and Switzerland. This puts E Bay far ahead of Amazon who had 11,6 Million visitors. Third place surprisingly goes to the price-comparison site Kelkoo with about 5 Million visitors. Then you have Expedia (2.5 Million) and Otto (2.2 Million).
