### What is AI content optimization?

AI content optimization is the practice of using AI tools to improve content and enhance its performance.

- ChatGPT to rewrite your content for readability
- Semrush’s AI Visibility Toolkit to research prompts your content should address

The performance gains you see from incorporating AI into your content processes could include getting more AI search citations, achieving higher traditional search rankings, and improving your user experience metrics.

### What are the benefits of AI content optimization?

- Improved content performance : By processing massive amounts of data at scale, AI tools may spot content optimization opportunities that you might’ve missed but can now act on
- Manual work reduction : AI tools streamline the optimization process by automating time-consuming tasks like keyword research and content analysis
- Faster idea generation and content editing : AI content solutions can identify relevant topics, grammatical errors, and stylistic inconsistencies much quicker than humans
- Scalability : AI content optimization tools help you expand content operations without incurring significantly more costs

#### 2. Optimize title tags and meta descriptions

Optimizing your title tags and meta descriptions is a good way to improve click-through rates in AI and traditional search results.

Title tags and meta descriptions are HTML elements that define page titles and summaries, respectively. AI tools and traditional search engines can use or reference both HTML elements to generate search results snippets

- Entice readers to click
- Incorporate your target query
- Be short to avoid truncation (i.e., fewer than 60 characters for title tags and 105 characters for meta descriptions)

Here’s a prompt you can enter into ChatGPT to generate optimized title tags and meta descriptions

"Generate title tags and meta descriptions for this article. The title tags and meta descriptions should entice readers to click and must include the ‘[keyword]’ keyword. The title tags should not exceed 60 characters, and the meta descriptions should not exceed 105 characters. Article: [text]"

Alternatively, use the Content Toolkit’s AI Article Generator to create title tags and meta descriptions for articles you generate with it. Enter your article idea into the tool, adjust the settings, and click “ Generate article .”

AI Article Generator will produce a search-friendly article that includes the title tag and meta description at the top. If you’re dissatisfied with either of these, you can regenerate them with AI or tweak them manually.

If you need optimized title tags and meta descriptions for existing drafts, use the Content Toolkit’s AI Search Optimizer .

"Generate title tags and meta descriptions for this article. The title tags and meta descriptions must include the ‘[keyword]’ keyword. The title tags and meta descriptions should not exceed 60 and 105 characters, respectively."

#### 4. Find internal linking opportunities

AI tools are useful for finding opportunities to add internal links between your site’s pages, which distributes page authority and helps people and search systems discover your content.

You can use ChatGPT to find internal linking opportunities by providing a list of your site’s key pages and asking for link suggestions. The more pages you list, the more links ChatGPT can suggest.

"Here are three key articles on my fitness website: ‘[article title 1],’ ‘[article title 2],’ and ‘[article title 3].’ Please suggest how I might link these articles internally."

Review the tool’s internal link suggestions, and then work on those you agree with.

#### 5. Analyze search intent

Analyze your target keyword’s or prompt’s search intent — what people are trying to achieve with their search — so you can create content that addresses it.

You can analyze search intent with ChatGPT. Open the tool, enable its web search feature, and then give it a prompt like this

“Search Google for the top 10 results for the keyword ‘[keyword].’ Based on what you find, identify the specific information searchers are looking for and explain your reasoning.”

#### 6. Enhance your writing’s readability

Having an AI tool suggest ways of improving your writing’s readability can make your content easier for people and search systems to understand.

ChatGPT can help review your writing with a prompt like this

“Please review this text for any jargon or complex sentences that my target audience might struggle to understand. Suggest simpler alternatives to improve clarity and readability. My target audience is [describe your target audience here]. Text: [full text].”

### Common AI content optimization mistakes to avoid

- Failing to fact-check AI outputs : AI tools are prone to hallucinations, which means they may provide misleading or nonsensical responses. Double-check the accuracy of AI-generated content before using it.
- Using generic AI content as is : Generic content won’t stand out from the competition. Enhance AI content with unique insights and data you’ve gained from real-world experience.
- Not tailoring AI content to your audience : AI-generated content may not resonate with your audience if it isn’t created with their characteristics in mind. Provide AI tools with details on your target audience so they can adapt their output to match.
- Not providing AI tools with tone of voice and style guides : Without guidance, AI tools may generate content in a tone and style incompatible with your brand’s. Give these tools tone and style requirements to follow when generating content.
- Keeping verbose AI content : AI-generated content may contain bloated explanations. Shorten these to make your writing more relevant and engaging.
- Acting on every AI recommendation : Doing this may lead to over-optimized content. For example, adding all AI-recommended internal links without context can make your content look spammy. Follow AI recommendations only if they make sense.

### How to optimize content for AI search

- Making your content easy to parse by using short paragraphs and incorporating heading and list formatting where appropriate
- Answering questions in headings directly to help AI tools match your answers to these questions
- Restating the subjects of sentences , which improves clarity compared to using pronouns like “it” and “they”
- Adding authoritative references and data to strengthen arguments
- Updating your content regularly because research shows that AI tools favor recent content

### FAQs

#### How can AI help identify content gaps for optimization?

AI tools can compare your content against competing pages and surface subtopics, questions, or angles you haven't covered.

The fastest way to do this is with ChatGPT. Share your URL alongside two or three competing URLs and ask it to perform a content gap analysis. You'll get a list of missing subtopics, unanswered questions, and sections worth adding.

Additionally, you can use Semrush's Keyword Gap tool to find keywords your competitors rank for that you don't. This is the sort of data that AI tools don’t have access to. Once you find the missing keywords, use them in your content to start showing up for the query your competitors do.

#### How do Google's helpful content guidelines apply to AI-generated content?

Google's guidelines focus on whether content is helpful to people, not on how it was produced. AI-generated content can rank well as long as it meets the same standards as high quality, human-written content. It has to be accurate, original, and useful.

Where AI content tends to run into trouble is when it's published as is. Generic AI output often lacks quality.

#### How do I optimize content for Google AI Overviews and AI summaries?

Google AI Overviews pull from pages that already perform well in traditional search, so strong SEO fundamentals are still a requirement. Your content has to be accessible, easy to parse, and genuinely useful.

- Check your robots.txt file is configured properly. Check your robots.txt file to make sure Google-Extended and GoogleBot (Google’s web crawlers) aren't blocked. If they are, your content has no chance of being referenced in AI summaries
- Structure your content for AI extraction. Use clear H2s and H3s that break up your content into focused sections. In each section, lead with the answer, takeaway, or main point before getting into the supporting details. And keep paragraphs short and to the point.
- Cover the full topic, not just the keyword. AI Overviews stitch together answers from multiple pages, so the more completely you address related sub-questions on one page, the more likely you are to be the source it pulls from
- Build topical authority. Google tends to pull content from sites that have demonstrated depth on a subject across multiple pieces of content
- Keep information fresh. AI Overviews lean toward recently updated pages, especially for anything time-sensitive
