## Extracted Body
A free AI visibility audit shows how often your brand appears in AI-generated answers when people ask questions about your category.

Whether you're managing your own brand's visibility or auditing a new client, AI systems like ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews are now part of how buyers discover and evaluate options

If your brand isn't showing up in those AI answers that are recommending options, competitors are winning that space instead.

For example, when someone asks AI about the best trail running shoes for someone with wide feet, which brands show up, and how often across similar prompts?

Here's how you can find out, using free Semrush tools, in less than 30 minutes.

These three checks will confirm whether AI visibility gaps are costing you awareness and consideration.

SEO audits and AI visibility audits measure different layers of the same goal: being found when people search. SEO audits focus on rankings, keyword gaps, and organic traffic.

AI visibility audits focus on how often your brand appears in AI-generated answers: mentions, citations, sentiment, and brand inclusion across platforms like ChatGPT, AI Overviews, and Gemini.

SEO audits measure how well a site ranks. AI visibility audits measure how likely your brand is to get recommended.

Both matter. Running them together reveals where strong SEO performance doesn't translate into AI visibility, and where AI systems cite sources that search rankings alone wouldn't predict.

Run a free AI visibility audit alongside your regular SEO audits , to track both layers of visibility.

With a free Semrush account, you can audit how your brand appears on AI in under 30 minutes.

Start with Domain Overview to see if your brand shows up in AI-generated responses.

These metrics provide a quick way to confirm whether a brand appears in AI-generated answers before moving into deeper analysis.

Use Site Audit to check whether technical issues are blocking AI platforms from crawling and citing your content.

Set up a Site Audit for your domain and run a crawl of up to 100 pages (the free plan limit).

Once the crawl completes, review your AI Search Health score in the overview dashboard.
