# Oncrawl opens offices in Montreal, Canada

## Summary

## Extracted Body
After Bordeaux and Paris, we are proud to announce the opening of Oncrawl’s office in Montreal! This new strategic installation will enable us to accelerate our development in North-America and bring us closer to our customers”, shares François Goube, CEO of Oncrawl. Bordeaux 17th, October 2017 – The French-based SEO solution Oncrawl accelerates its expansion and opens its offices in Montreal, Canada

With already 35% of its turnover generated abroad, the Oncrawl solution is now officially deployed in the North American market. The company wants to make Montreal a key hub for its development and plans to hire over an 100 people for its three offices, mostly engineers, business developers and customer success managers.

In addition to its strategic geographic location, Montreal is one of the world’s major hub for big data and artificial intelligence companies, while the entire Canadian digital marketplace is booming. We already have many customers on the east coast and we want to get closer to them to improve customer support and customer relationship, confides François Goube

Laura Bony, French expatriate in Montréal for 5 years, has joined Oncrawl as VP Sales for North America. Former account executive and digital service manager for Rézoway, a service helping French companies to establish in Canada, she will be in charge of making Oncrawl the first SEO solution in Canada and the United States. She has a strong interest in business development and SaaS solutions and will be Oncrawl’s voice locally. While Oncrawl is set to conquer the North American market, the company does not intend to relocate its Bordeaux-based technology pole.

The tool that helps e-commerce sites and online media to improve their traffic, revenues and rankings in Google results, aims to become the world’s leading search engine optimization solution. Today, the company has more than 500 customers in Europe and North America such as L’Oréal, Merkle, Mondadori, Ssense, Canon Europe or the first e-commerce website in Poland, Skapiec. Oncrawl has also just won a call for tenders with the number 1 e-commerce company in France, Cdiscount. With its 15 million references, the merchant site needed a tool capable of quickly and comprehensively analyzing its performance in terms of SEO. The technologies and solutions proposed, the support and proximity, the group being also based in Bordeaux, have made Oncrawl the ideal partner.
