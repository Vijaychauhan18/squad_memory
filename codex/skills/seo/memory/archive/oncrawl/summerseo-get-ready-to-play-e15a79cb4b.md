Summer is here! Some lucky ones can’t wait until their upcoming sunny holidays but some others are staying behind their desks to keep maintaining the SEO activity. In order to support these heroes and to entertain them a little bit, we have been thinking about a special Summer SEO Game. Every week, we will release a GIF featuring a summer scene. Your job is to reproduce the scene with your team or by yourself, take a picture of it and post it on the comments. One participant will win a SEO gift offer by our partners.
