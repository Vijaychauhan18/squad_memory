## Extracted Article Passages
- The Detailed SEO Extension now has over 520,000 weekly users , which I’m incredibly grateful for.
- Now, as part of Ahrefs, we want to keep making the extension better and add the most useful features for SEOs and digital marketers.
- Alongside some small visual tweaks and important backend code improvements, here are the newest features in the latest release.
- You might not know this, but the Detailed SEO Extension has a “hidden” Right > Click menu.
- Simply hover over any text on the web, Right > Click, and the extension will let you find other places that content has been posted.
- “On this site” automatically appends a site: search operator with the correct domain, or ignores it to search for the entire web.
- I use this during site audits to see how templated sites or, and if anyone is outranking a site using content they’ve copied.
- We’ve always had a Social tab, but it only showed you the URL of any social card data a page might have in place.
- Now, thanks to this latest update, you the actual social details with load, along with their titles and descriptions.
- Platforms typically rely on the Open Graph protocol, but some sites do also allow their own tags, such as Twitter. #3
- Instead of just being able to download the images on a page, you can now see a preview thumbnail of each one, and whether or not they’re missing Alt Text.
- Google’s own documentation on image SEO recommends images have descriptive alt text as it’s something they use to help understand the subject matter of an image. #4
- While this is only a small update, it has been requested by our users to speed up finding where a page is linking to.
- Now, on the Links tab, there are separate tabs for both Internal and External links, rather than having them in one long, continous list.
- This is especially useful when there are a lot of internal links on a page, so you don’t need to scroll so far to find the external ones.
- In September of 2025 I shared the news that Detailed has been acquired by Ahrefs, and I would be joining the company to help with marketing and product development.
