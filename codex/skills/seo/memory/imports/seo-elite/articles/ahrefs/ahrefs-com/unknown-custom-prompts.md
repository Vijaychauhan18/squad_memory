## Extracted Article Passages
- These are Custom Prompts in Brand Radar. And with them, you can monitor how AI tools respond to your own specific prompts.
- Now, Brand Radar already tracks your brand mentions across 380 million search-backed prompts — visible under the AI responses report. Think of it as a discovery tool that maps the breadth of your AI funnel, showing you the topics and prompts you could be found for.
- Custom prompts add a second layer of depth . You pick the exact questions your buyers ask about your product, your competitors, your category; and Brand Radar tracks how ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Copilot respond to each one.
- If you don’t have one yet, click + Report and fill in your brand and competitors. Set the tracking frequency for custom prompts — daily, weekly or monthly — for each AI tool separately. And click Create report.
- From here, click Tracked prompts in the sidebar. If you already know what prompts to use, add them here, one per line.
- Before you confirm, check the estimated check count. One check equals one prompt times one AI tool times one location. So 10 prompts across 4 AI tools in 2 locations — that’s 80 checks.
- Finally, click Add prompts . The first AI visibility data should appear within 24 hours.
- Not sure which prompts to track? Click on Generate to generate them from competitor comparisons, pricing data, People also ask, Reddit and forum discussions, and other signals Ahrefs collects.
- One tip: don’t obsess over individual responses since they can change from one minute to the next. Instead, group similar custom prompts together and look at the bigger picture.
- This evens out the data and gives you a clear picture of how much market your own.
- 1. Competitive positioning . Questions like “what’s the best CRM for real estate agents” or “what’s the best CRM for construction companies.” These show how AI tools rank you against competitors for your key use cases.
- 2. Trust and validation . Questions people ask in forums and communities. “Are Hoka running shoes reliable long-term” or “is HubSpot worth it for a small business.” These reveal what AI thinks about your brand’s reputation.
- 3. And specific requirements . Niche queries like “best HIPAA-compliant project management tool” or “which CRM integrates with Xero.” These go beyond broad coverage and let you track exactly what your buyers are asking.
- Once you’ve added your custom prompts, it’s worth noting that you can toggle between them and Ahrefs’ AI visibility index.
- Just go to the Overview report, or any of the AI visibility reports in the sidebar, and set the Dataset filter to Tracked prompts only . You’ll see how each AI tool answers these and whether they mention you, your competitors, or skip you entirely.
- Curious how many prompts you have left? You can check that anytime under Account Settings > Limits and Usage.
