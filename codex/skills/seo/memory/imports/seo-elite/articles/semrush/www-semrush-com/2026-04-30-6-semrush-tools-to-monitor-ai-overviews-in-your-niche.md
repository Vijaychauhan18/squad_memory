## Why This Matters
Learn how to use six Semrush tools to monitor AI Overviews, track brand citations, benchmark competitors, and improve your visibility across AIOs.

## Extracted Article Passages
- Google’s AI Overviews (AIOs) now reach more than two billion monthly users across 200+ countries.
- Which means it’s critical to identify which queries trigger these summaries, how your brand shows up, and which competitors are being cited.
- Semrush One offers six tools to monitor AI Overviews and improve your brand visibility.
- Here’s how to use them for everything from monitoring how frequently AIOs appear for your target keywords to conducting an industry-wide trend analysis.
- Note: For enterprise-level businesses, Semrush Enterprise AIO offers all of the following capabilities with higher limits and more support.
- The Organic Rankings tool shows how often AI Overviews appear across the keywords you already rank for.
- For any pages that are ranking as a traditional search result but not in the AI Overview, look at the sources being cited to understand how they answer the query. Then improve your content to better address that intent.
- Tip: Click “KD” > “Easy,” and select “SERP Features,” hover over “Domain doesn’t rank,” and click “AI Overview” to find low-competition keywords where you’re already ranking but not yet cited. These are strong opportunities to gain visibility in AI Overviews with targeted updates.
- The Position Tracking tool shows how often AI Overviews appear across a custom set of target keywords and whether your domain is included.
- Unlike Organic Rankings, it’s based on a keyword set you define. You can group keywords by priority and monitor changes with daily updates.
- This lets you track the keywords and topics that matter most to your business (even if you’re not ranking for them yet) and see how AI Overviews affect those results over time.
- Tracking both how frequently AI Overviews appear, and then how often your domain is cited within them helps you see how well your strategy is helping you attain AI visibility .
- The amount of keywords you can track in Position Tracking depends on your Semrush subscription level . And with Semrush One, you can track ChatGPT visibility alongside AI Overviews and traditional Google rankings.
- The Visibility Overview report gives you a topic-level snapshot of your brand’s presence in AI Overviews.
