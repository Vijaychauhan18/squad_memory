## Extracted Article Passages
- For global brands, AI citation is binary: your brand is either mentioned when a buyer asks “what is the best X for Y,” or it is invisible to an increasingly large segment of your acquisition funnel. Nearly 60% of search queries now end without a click to any website, according to SparkToro research , and AI-generated answers are accelerating that trend. Enterprise buyers increasingly start research inside ChatGPT and Perplexity before they ever reach a traditional search result.
- This shift is creating real confusion for decision-makers evaluating AI search optimization partners, as one practitioner noted on r/digital_marketing :
- “Has anyone else noticed your organic traffic dipping even though your SEO rankings seem fine? Lately it feels like more people are just asking ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google’s AI stuff directly instead of clicking through to websites. The annoying part is I have no idea: How often my brand gets mentioned in these AI answers, Whether it’s actually bringing in traffic or leads, What people are even saying about my brand.”
- If you are building a shortlist of AI search optimization partners, you have likely already encountered a structural problem: most recommendations, including those generated by AI systems themselves, conflate AI SEO software platforms (BrightEdge, Conductor, Botify) with AI SEO service agencies. These are fundamentally different categories. Platforms are tools your team already owns or can license. Agencies are the strategic and execution partners who operationalize AI search strategy within your existing tech stack, engineering workflows, and multi-market architecture. This evaluation covers service agencies specializing in AI search optimization, also referred to as generative engine optimization (GEO) or answer engine optimization (AEO), exclusively.
- If your current agency cannot explain how AI systems retrieve and cite information, or cannot show you where competitors are earning citations that your brand is not, the decision to evaluate specialized partners is the correct one. The framework below is designed to make that evaluation systematic.
- These are the criteria we applied. They are also the criteria you should apply during your own agency evaluation calls.
- Multi-Platform AI Monitoring Depth : AI visibility spans Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. Each platform uses different LLM retrieval mechanics and citation behaviors. Single-platform optimization leaves most AI-influenced buyer journeys unaddressed.
- Engineering Integration and Technical Architecture Capability : JavaScript-heavy, headless, and multi-domain international architectures require sprint-embedded execution, not strategy decks. Crawlability, rendering, and entity signal fragmentation directly determine whether AI systems can extract brand information.
- Competitive AI Gap Analysis Methodology : Enterprise stakeholders need an AI visibility audit that shows precisely where competitors earn citations while their brand is absent, segmented by journey stage, market, and revenue impact.
- Entity Architecture and Structured Data Ecosystems : Fragmented entity signals across international domains cause AI systems to misrepresent or omit brands entirely. Entity clarity is the prerequisite for reliable citation.
- AI-Specific Measurement and KPI Framework : Citation rate, surface presence, recommendation share, and competitive citation gap are the metrics that matter. Traditional rankings and traffic do not capture AI search performance.
- Verifiable Enterprise Client Caliber and Global Scale : Multi-market complexity, international architectures, and distributed engineering teams require institutional experience that single-market agencies lack.
- We weighted multi-platform AI monitoring depth and engineering integration capability highest because these directly determine whether AI systems can extract and cite brand information, the prerequisite for every other optimization. An agency with strong content strategy but no capability to resolve JavaScript rendering failures or entity fragmentation cannot deliver AI visibility for architecturally complex global sites.
- AI search measurement is still maturing. No industry-standard benchmarks exist yet for citation rate or recommendation share. The criteria below reflect current practitioner evidence; they will evolve as the field does. If your organization has not yet established AI-specific KPIs, that is normal. The agencies worth hiring will help you build that measurement framework rather than assuming you already have one.
- These six criteria, multi-platform monitoring, engineering integration, competitive gap analysis, entity architecture, AI-specific measurement, and enterprise client caliber, form the evaluation framework applied identically to every agency in this assessment.
- Disclosure: This evaluation is published by Onely, which appears at position #1. We applied identical evaluation criteria to every agency, verified competitor information through independent sources, and present competitor strengths without minimization. The vetting questions later in this article are designed to evaluate any agency on this list, including us.
