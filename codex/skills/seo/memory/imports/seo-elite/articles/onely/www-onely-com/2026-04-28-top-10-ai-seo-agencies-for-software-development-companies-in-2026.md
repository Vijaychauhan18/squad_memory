- If you have an established SEO program that is plateauing while AI surfaces grow, the challenge is not your SEO fundamentals; it is that the optimization target has shifted. AI-Powered SEO uses AI tools to execute conventional optimization – content generation, keyword clustering, automated audits. AI Search Optimization re-architects your digital ecosystem so AI platforms can extract, attribute, and cite your products in conversational answers. This discipline – variously called Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), or AI Search Optimization – requires fundamentally different capabilities than traditional SEO. Most agencies offer the former. Software companies with complex architectures need the latter.
- This guide ranks 10 agencies based on what actually matters for software companies with JavaScript-heavy, headless, or API-first architectures: AI search optimization depth, technical stack compatibility, entity architecture capabilities, engineering workflow integration, vertical specialization, and AI citation monitoring.
